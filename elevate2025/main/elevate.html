About the Event

Hosted by SALIHIN Academy, Elevate 2025 is a knowledge-sharing platform designed to empower the next generation of accountants and advisors. With a forward-looking agenda, this event targets finance professionals, students, and industry leaders eager to thrive in an evolving digital and ethical landscape.
